About

Welcome to my blog. I’m a data and visualization enthusiast working in research and development. In my free time, I try to keep up with the development in data and analytics fields.

I started this blog because I needed a way to document my learnings. In my work and personal projects I encounter things like new tools and methods all the time. I wanted to have a place to record what I have done. And why not to share with others!

In the beginning, I will post about R programming, data visualization and machine learning tools for Azure cloud.
